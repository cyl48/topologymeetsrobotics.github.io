Themes of Interest

This workshop aims to discuss the following topics:
  • Application of topological methods in problems generally related to robotics
  • Topological tools and representations for:
    • interpretable/explainable AI
    • human-friendly abstractions of complex systems
    • formal verification of robotic systems
    • motion/path (and/or task) planning
    • SLAM
    • state estimation and trajectory prediction
  • Design of metrics for robotics and human-robot interaction using topological tools
  • Software packages/libraries with topological tools
